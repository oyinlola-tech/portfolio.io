My Journey

From Passion to Profession

My tech journey began with a fascination for how websites work. As a self-taught developer, I earned certifications in frontend development while simultaneously training as a virtual assistant. This unique combination allows me to approach projects with both technical expertise and organizational efficiency.

While building my development skills, I discovered cybersecurity through a friend who worked in penetration testing. The idea of protecting digital assets resonated with me deeply. A month ago, I began formal cybersecurity training to complement my development skills.

What drives me is the belief that beautiful interfaces should also be secure interfaces. I'm currently deepening my knowledge in networking fundamentals, threat analysis, and security best practices to become a well-rounded tech professional.
