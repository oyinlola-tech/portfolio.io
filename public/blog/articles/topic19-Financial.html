Financial Literacy: Why It Should Be a Core Subject in Schools

Simple curriculum ideas to teach budgeting, saving, debt and basic investing early.

Financial literacy equips students with skills to make informed decisions: budgeting, managing debt, understanding interest, and basic investing. These skills prevent costly mistakes later and increase financial independence.

Core topics to teach

  • Budgeting and goal-setting
  • Interest, loans, and debt management
  • Saving and basic investing principles
  • Taxes and simple consumer protection

Classroom activities

  • Simulate a monthly budget and prioritize spending.
  • Create a classroom marketplace to teach earning and saving.
  • Track a hypothetical investment over time to learn about compounding.

Takeaway
