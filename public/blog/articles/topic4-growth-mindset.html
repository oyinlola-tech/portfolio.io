What's a Growth Mindset?

Belief that abilities can be developed through effort and strategies, rather than fixed traits. It encourages risk-taking and learning from mistakes.

Practical Moves

  • Praise effort and strategies, not only outcomes.
  • Model "I don't know yet" instead of "I can't".
  • Make failure a learning task: what did the action teach you?

Teacher/Parent Tips

Provide scaffolded challenges and feedback focused on progress. Encourage reflection: what improved and what next?

Takeaway

Value progress over perfection. Growth mindset creates lifelong learners.
